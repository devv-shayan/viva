# Viva - Team Ownership

This document records the current ownership model, not a dated build schedule.

## Dev A - AI and conversation pipeline

- OpenAI analysis, assessment, and dossier contracts
- Realtime conversation integration
- Evidence validation and server-side safety rules
- Core test coverage for model and conversation behavior

## Dev B - Product experience and delivery

- Teacher and student workspaces
- Class, invite, upload, and report workflows
- Cloudflare D1/R2 integration and deployment readiness
- Accessibility, UI consistency, documentation, QA, and demo production

## Shared responsibilities

- Keep the teacher as the final academic decision-maker.
- Review changes that affect the evidence record, roles, or permissions.
- Verify the judge demo and end-to-end classroom flow before release.
- Keep secrets out of the repository and deployment logs.
